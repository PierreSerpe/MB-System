Detailed Description

Macro definitions to swap 2-byte and 4-byte integers between big-endian and little-endian machines.

Macro definitions used to swap bytes for 2 and 4 byte integer values to deal with the differences between "big endian" and "little endian" machines. The two macros mb_swap_short and mb_swap_long come courtesy of Paul Cohen of Sonatech.
